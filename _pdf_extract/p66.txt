Action Verbs
VERB DEFINITION
PURGE Get rid of or eliminate.
Force flow through an enclosed volume.
PUSH Move equipment or actuate equipment away from motivating force.
RACK IN Place an electrical circuit breaker in place by physically connecting it to associated power
source.
RACK OUT Disconnect an electrical circuit breaker by physically removing from associated power
source.
RAISE Increase the amount; move to higher position.
RECORD Document specified condition data or characteristic.
REDUCE Cause a parameter to decrease in value.
REFURBISH Brighten or freshen up; make like new.
REMOVE Transfer or move, take off, out of, or away.
REPEAT Say or do again.
REPLACE Substitute; put back into former position or place.
REQUEST Ask for.
RESET Remove an active output signal from retentive logic device even with the input signal still
present.
RESTORE Bring a specified parameter or component back within specified limits or original condition
RETRACT Draw back.
RETURN Move backwards, bring, go, or send.
SAMPLE Take a representative portion for the purpose of examination.
SECURE Fasten, make safe, or tie; remove from service; stop a device.
SELECT Choose from among several.
SET Physically adjust and adjustable feature to a specified value.
SHAKE Move or cause to go to and from in a quick jerky manner; agitate.
SHIELD Provide with protective cover or barrier.
SHIFT Change mode of operation, usually speed.
SHUT DOWN Stop operating equipment and place in standby.
START Initiate operation of an electrical or mechanical device either directly or by remote control.
STOP Shut down equipment, terminate operation, or cease.
STROKE Operate a valve over its full travel.
Go through a motion as in a valve or damper movement.
SURVEY Investigate an area, generally for radiological purposes.
SYNCHRONIZE Make exactly simultaneous in operation.

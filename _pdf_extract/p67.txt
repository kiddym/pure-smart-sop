Action Verbs
VERB DEFINITION
TAG Label for identification.
THROTTLE Operate a valve in an intermediate position to obtain certain flow rate up to and including
full open.
TORQUE Apply turning or twisting force to specified value.
TRIP Manually activate or deactivate a semiautomatic feature.
TROUBLESHOOT Investigate for purpose of locating or eliminating sources of problem.
TURN Cause to move at an angle or circle.
USE Put into action or service; employ.
VENT Permit a gas or liquid contained under pressure to escape from a vent.
VERIFY Observe an expected condition exist (no actions to correct).
WITHDRAW Move from a position.

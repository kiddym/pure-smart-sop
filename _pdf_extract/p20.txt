4.13 Procedure Structure and Sections
4.13.1 General Requirements
1. Use a standard procedure structure that is consistently maintained for all
procedure types.
2. The following Table 1 designates the required (R) and optional (O) sections for
each procedure type and lists these sections in the recommended order:

Procedure Section

Table 1, Procedure Structure
3. Do not omit required sections. Use None or NA when no information is needed
for a required section.
4. Some sections may be combined; however, address the intent of each section.
